Schedule

Monday

On monday class runs from 7-8:30 pm and is located at Goldsmiths's Richard Hoggart building,
in room RHB300.This class is lead by the coaches from Aquillaand varies between being a traditional poomsae class
or a sparring class. afterwards there is a society social held at a local pub(drinking is not mandatory).

Adress

8 Lewisham Way, London SE14 6NW

Tuesday

On Tuesday class runs from 5-7 pm and is located at ual:london college of fashion, Holborn.This class
is student led, meaning if there is a specific focus you wish to train (poomsae,sparring,kicks,punches,etc.),
let the commitee member leading the class know and they will do their best to accomodate your request. In the past
we have even ran classes on grappling.

Adress

272 High Holborn, London WC1V 7EY

Friday

This class is held in the sports hall at Pendergrast Vale scool in Lewisham. This is the full Aquilla experience,
friday classes are taught by the Aquilla coaches and run from 6-8pm. The first hour is traditional Taekwondo, mostly poomsae,
the second hour is sparring, there is still a smaller poomsae class that runs in this hour however. This class is not student exclusive
meaning you'll be training along side other members of Aquilla Taekwondo.

Adress

Elmira St, London SE13 7BN
