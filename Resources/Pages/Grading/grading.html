Grading

What you will need for grading

An important part of your Taekwondo journey is your gradings. this is the method in which
you are assecsed on your current ability. the requirements for each belt follow a strict
curriculum. Once the the coaches believe you are ready you will be invited to do a grading,
depending on the belt you are grading for, will determine what you will need to pass.
see below...

Theory

  • Why do we bow in Taekwondo? To show respect
  • Do we kick with our toes or top of the foot? Top of the foot
  • Where does TKD come from? Korea
  • What is the Meaning of white belt? Innocence
